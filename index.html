About REPULSE

Repulse — an 19-year-old electronic music producer from Funen, Denmark. Over the past few years, I’ve been diving deep into the scene, and in just the last six months alone, my music has reached over 5 million streams on Spotify.

I take a lot of inspiration from artists like Robbe and Neptunica, and I love experimenting with different sounds to create something fresh and energetic. My tracks have been signed on big record labels like Kontor, Armada, Hot Vibes, Gahara, Relavel, Actuation, and more. I’m always working on pushing my sound to the next level.

I’ve gotten support from major labels like Spinnin' Records, Revealed, and artists like Timmy Trumpet, Klaas, and BENNETT.

For me, it’s all about connecting with people through music — and this is just the beginning.
